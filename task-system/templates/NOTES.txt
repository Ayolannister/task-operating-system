1. Install or upgrade the chart into AKS:

   helm upgrade --install task-system . --create-namespace --namespace task-system

2. Check resources:

   kubectl get pods,svc -n task-system

3. If frontend is exposed with a LoadBalancer:

   kubectl get svc -n task-system {{ include "task-system.fullname" . }}-frontend

4. To enable ingress, set `ingress.enabled: true` and update `ingress.hosts[0].host` to your domain.
